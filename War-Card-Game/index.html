Rules

  1. Click to reveal cards or continue
  2. Player with the highest number cards wins and get to keep its card
  3. winning card is put back in winners deck at the bottom
  4. Win a player reaches 0 cards in deck and did not win current match will lose the game
  5. Number cards are worth their respective number
  6. J,Q,K,A are worth 11,12,13,14 respectivally
  7. If there is a tie then both cards are discarded
