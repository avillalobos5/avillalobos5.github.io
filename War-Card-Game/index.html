Rules

  1. Click to reveal cards or to continue
  2. Player with the highest number card wins and get to keep their card
  3. The winning card is put back in winners deck
  4. When a player reaches 0 cards in deck and did not win current match, will lose the game
  5. Number cards are worth their respective number
  6. J,Q,K,A are worth 11,12,13,14 respectivally
  7. If there is a tie then both cards are discarded
